The Process

From Hello to Powered On — in 4 Steps

Most homeowners go from first conversation to system activation in just 4–8 weeks. We do the heavy lifting at every stage.

01

Free Consultation

We review your energy usage and design a custom solar plan tailored to your home and budget. No pressure, no sales tricks — just an honest look at what solar can do for you.

02

Custom System Design

Our engineering team designs a system optimized for your roof orientation, your location's sun exposure, and your family's actual energy needs. You'll see the design before we touch anything.

03

We Handle Permits & Paperwork

We manage all building permits, utility interconnection applications, and HOA approvals on your behalf. You don't lift a finger — we handle the bureaucracy so you don't have to.

04

Professional Install & Activation

Certified technicians install your system — typically in just one day. Once the utility approves the interconnection, we activate your system and you start generating clean solar power.

⏱ What's the Timeline?

Most R&K Energized installations are completed within 4 to 8 weeks from your initial consultation. The biggest variable is utility interconnection approval — some utilities move faster than others. We'll keep you updated every step of the way.

  • ✓ Week 1–2: Consultation & design
  • ✓ Week 2–4: Permits & utility application
  • ✓ Week 4–6: Installation day (usually 1 day)
  • ✓ Week 6–8: Utility inspection & system activation

Financing Options

A Plan for Every Budget

Whether you want $0 down, full ownership, or just reliable backup power — we have a program that works for you.

Common Questions

Solar Questions, Answered

How long does it take to go solar?

Most R&K Energized installations are complete within 4 to 8 weeks from your first consultation. The biggest variable is how quickly your utility approves the interconnection.

Do I need money up front to install solar?

No. We offer $0 down options including solar leases and Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs), so most homeowners start saving with no upfront cost. Loan and full-ownership plans are also available.

Who handles the permits and utility paperwork?

We do. R&K Energized manages all building permits, utility interconnection applications, and HOA approvals on your behalf — you don't lift a finger.

What areas does R&K Energized serve?

We install residential solar across California's Central Valley, including Fresno, Stockton, Bakersfield, Modesto, Tracy, Visalia, Hanford, and Kern County.
