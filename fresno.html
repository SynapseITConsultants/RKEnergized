Why Fresno Homeowners Are Going Solar

Fresno is one of the sunniest cities in California — with over 300 days of sunshine per year, it's an ideal location for residential solar. R&K Energized works with Fresno homeowners to design custom solar systems that maximize energy production given the Central Valley's intense summer sun and mild winters.

PG&E rates in Fresno have climbed significantly in recent years, and many homeowners are paying $200–$400 per month during summer cooling season. Solar gives you a predictable, lower rate locked in for years — so when utility rates go up, your solar rate stays steady. Most Fresno homeowners we work with see savings of 30–70% on their monthly electric bill starting in the first month their system is active.

With $0 down financing options including solar leases and PPAs, there's no barrier to getting started. We handle permits, utility interconnection with PG&E, and installation — typically in 4–8 weeks from your first call. Fresno's long sunny seasons mean your system will generate strong returns year-round.
