Solar Lease

A great choice for homeowners who want instant savings with zero hassle

With our Solar Lease, you get a fully installed solar system on your home for $0 down. We own and maintain the panels — you simply pay a fixed, predictable monthly rate that is lower than what you're currently paying your utility. No surprise spikes, no maintenance bills, no worries.

→ $0 upfront cost — panels installed at no charge
→ Fixed monthly payment, typically 20–40% below your current bill
→ We own, insure, monitor, and maintain the system
→ 25-year power production guarantee
→ Option to purchase system, upgrade, or transfer at end of term
→ Compatible with home battery backup

Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

Our most popular plan — pay only for what your panels produce, at a rate lower than the utility

The PPA is another $0 down option where instead of paying a flat lease rate, you pay a per-kilowatt-hour rate for the solar electricity your system produces — and that rate is lower than what your utility charges. When your panels produce more power, you pay more; when it's cloudy, you pay less. We own and maintain everything.

→ $0 upfront — panels installed and maintained by us
→ You pay a per-kWh rate (typically 10–30% below utility rates)
→ Great fit for homeowners with variable or seasonal energy usage
→ We handle all monitoring, maintenance, and repairs
→ 25-year agreement with price escalator cap
→ Available in most service areas

Solar Loan

Own your system from day one — and keep every penny of the tax credits

With a Solar Loan, you finance the purchase of your system with manageable monthly payments — and you own the panels outright. That means you're eligible for the federal solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC), which can be worth 30% of your system cost. Ownership also means your home equity grows and you sell the system with your house if you move.

→ Low monthly payments — often less than your current electric bill
→ You own the system from day one
→ Eligible for 30% Federal Solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC)*
→ We still provide monitoring and warranty support
→ Increases home resale value
→ Once paid off — your power is essentially free

Home Battery Backup

Keep your home powered when the grid goes down — day or night

Home battery storage is available as an add-on to any solar program. It stores excess energy your panels produce during the day so you can use it at night or during a power outage. For homeowners who've lived through grid failures, a home battery provides something money can't put a price on: peace of mind.

→ Stores solar energy for use at night or during outages
→ Keeps critical appliances running when the grid fails
→ Professionally managed and monitored 24/7
→ Can reduce demand charges in time-of-use utility markets
→ Available with Lease, PPA, Loan, or Cash purchase
→ Qualifies for additional incentives in many states
