Why Battery Backup

Power When You Need It Most

A home battery stores the solar energy your panels produce during the day so you can use it at night, during peak-rate hours, or when the grid goes down. For families who've lived through outages, that's priceless peace of mind.

🔌

Backup Power During Outages
