Why Modesto Homeowners Are Going Solar

Modesto sits in the heart of California's Central Valley, where hot summers and PG&E's tiered rate structure create some of the highest residential energy bills in the region. Homeowners in Stanislaus County are increasingly turning to solar as a reliable, long-term solution — locking in a lower energy rate before the next inevitable utility increase.

R&K Energized designs each solar system specifically for the Modesto climate and your home's unique roof layout. We handle everything from your initial energy assessment through permits, PG&E interconnection, and installation. Most Modesto homeowners are up and generating clean solar power within 4–8 weeks of their first conversation with us — and many see a lower energy cost from their very first bill.

Modesto's growing demand for home electrification — heat pumps, EV chargers, and smart home upgrades — makes pairing solar with a SPAN smart panel or home battery an especially smart investment. We'll help you plan a system that handles today's needs and scales with your home's future.
