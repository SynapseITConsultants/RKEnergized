Solar Installation in Bakersfield, CA

When Kern County summers hit triple digits, your AC shouldn't break the bank. R&K Energized installs solar — and optional battery backup — that keeps Bakersfield homes cool, powered, and saving. $0 down options available.

Why Bakersfield Homeowners Are Going Solar

Bakersfield is one of the sunniest and hottest cities in California — summer temperatures regularly exceed 100°F, and cooling costs can push electric bills above $300 per month for many Kern County homeowners. That extreme sun is exactly what makes Bakersfield one of the best places in the state to go solar. Your panels work hardest during the exact months your bills are highest.

R&K Energized serves homeowners throughout Bakersfield and Kern County, working with both PG&E and SCE-served properties. We design each system based on your home's roof layout, energy usage, and local utility rate structure to maximize your savings from day one. With $0 down lease and PPA options, most Bakersfield homeowners can start saving on their very first bill with no upfront cost.

Battery backup is especially popular in Kern County, where summer heat waves occasionally stress the grid. Adding a Tesla Powerwall 3 or Lunar Battery means your home stays powered and cool even during a grid outage — giving your family real peace of mind when temperatures soar.
