About R&K Energized

We're Your Neighbors in Solar

R&K Energized is an independent, locally operated solar sales business built on one belief: every homeowner deserves access to clean, affordable energy — without the corporate runaround. We bring Sunrun's world-class solar directly to your door with the personal service you actually want.

Our Story

Built by Entrepreneurs,
For Homeowners

R&K Energized — operating as R&K Group — was founded by Raymond with a simple vision: build a business that actually helps people. The solar industry is full of companies focused on volume and commissions. We're focused on you.

As an independent authorized dealer for Sunrun, we have the freedom to give every homeowner our full attention. We're not a call center or a corporate sales floor. We're a hands-on team that generates leads, builds real relationships through door-to-door conversations and community outreach, and guides families through the solar process from first conversation to activation day.

We chose to partner with Sunrun because they're simply the best — the #1 residential solar company in America, with 1 million+ customers, a 25-year power guarantee, and industry-leading support. That means you get the personal service of a small business backed by the power of the nation's largest solar company.
