About R&K Energized

We're Your Neighbors in Solar

R&K Energized is an independent, locally operated solar business built on one belief: every homeowner deserves access to clean, affordable energy — without the corporate runaround. We bring world-class solar directly to your door with the personal service you actually want.

Our Story

Built by Entrepreneurs,
For Homeowners

R&K Energized — operating as R&K Group — was founded by Raymond with a simple vision: build a business that actually helps people. The solar industry is full of companies focused on volume and commissions. We're focused on you.

We have the freedom to give every homeowner our full attention. We're not a call center or a corporate sales floor. We're a hands-on team that builds real relationships through door-to-door conversations and community outreach, and guides families through the solar process from first conversation to activation day.

We work only with industry-leading equipment and installation partners — because your home and your investment deserve the best. That means you get the personal service of a local business paired with quality you can count on for decades.
